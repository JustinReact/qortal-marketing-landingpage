# Groups on Qortal ü§ù

Qortal groups allow users to create communities on the blockchain.

## What Are Groups?
Groups let users:
- Organize discussions.
- Share content.
- Build decentralized communities.

## Creating a Group
1. Open Qortal and go to **Groups**.
2. Click **Create Group**.
3. Set a name and description.
4. Click **Submit** to register it on the blockchain.

## Managing Group Members
### Adding Members
1. Open the group page.
2. Click **Invite Member** and enter their Qortal ID.
3. Approve the request.

### Removing Members
1. Open the group settings.
2. Click **Remove** next to a user.
